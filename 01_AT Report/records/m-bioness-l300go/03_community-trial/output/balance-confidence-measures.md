Total ABC score / % self-confidence: **left blank on the form** — needs a clinician to sum and calculate (16 items, /1600 → percentage; standard cutoff for high fall risk is commonly cited around <50-67% depending on the population studied — don't cite a specific threshold without checking the appropriate reference for MS).

**This is the most striking of the three instruments** — Megan reports actively avoiding nearly every daily and community activity listed *specifically because of fear of falling*, at "Agree" or "Completely agree" for 13 of 14 items. This is direct, dated, signed documentary evidence for exactly the claim `report.md` already makes qualitatively in multiple places (e.g. Executive Summary: *"Megan actively modifies her activities, walking environments and daily routines in order to maintain safety"*) — but the report currently doesn't cite this instrument at all.

## Why this matters for the report
