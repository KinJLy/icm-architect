환자 근육스트렝스 테스트 결과가 0/5 와 3/5 가있어 0은 피지오가 테스트 한거구 3은 뉴롤로지스트가 한거야. 최종본 작성해놓은데 전체적인 수정보다 관련부분 수정할수 있어?

네. 이 경우에는 전체 레포트를 다시 작성할 필요는 없고, 관련된 부분만 수정하는 것이 좋습니다.

특히 NDIS 심사관 입장에서는 같은 환자에게 DF strength가 0/5와 3/5로 기록되어 있으면 "자료가 서로 상충된다"고 볼 수 있으므로, 왜 차이가 나는지 설명을 추가하는 것이 중요합니다.

수정해야 할 섹션
1. Assessment Findings (Strength Assessment)

기존:

Right ankle dorsiflexion strength was assessed as 0/5.

수정:

Ankle dorsiflexion strength has been reported differently across assessments. During physiotherapy assessment, dorsiflexion strength was recorded as 0/5, indicating no observable active movement against gravity. In comparison, neurological assessment documented dorsiflexion strength as 3/5, indicating active movement against gravity but reduced functional control. Variability between assessments may reflect fluctuations commonly associated with Multiple Sclerosis, differences in assessment conditions, fatigue levels, examiner interpretation, and the distinction between isolated muscle testing and functional gait performance.

Regardless of the recorded muscle grade, clinically significant foot drop was consistently observed during gait assessment, resulting in impaired foot clearance, increased tripping risk, and reduced walking efficiency.

2. Clinical Assessment Summary

Strength Table이 있다면:

Muscle Group	Physiotherapy Assessment	Neurology Assessment
Ankle Dorsiflexion	0/5	3/5
Plantarflexion	3/5	3/5
Eversion	0/5	0/5
Inversion	1/5	1/5

표 아래에 추가:

Variability in dorsiflexion strength measurements was noted between clinical assessments. Functional gait assessment demonstrated persistent foot drop regardless of the measured muscle grade. Clinical recommendations were therefore based primarily on functional performance, safety considerations, and community mobility outcomes rather than isolated muscle strength scores.

3. Trial Outcomes Section

여기에 한 문단 추가하는 것이 좋습니다.

While dorsiflexion strength measurements varied between assessors (0/5 to 3/5), functional gait deficits remained evident throughout the assessment process. The participant continued to demonstrate inadequate foot clearance during swing phase, recurrent tripping risk, and increased fatigue during ambulation. The observed improvements during Bioness L300 Go trials therefore support the clinical need for functional electrical stimulation regardless of isolated manual muscle testing results.

4. Clinical Justification Section

이 부분이 가장 중요합니다.

추가:

The recommendation for Bioness L300 Go is based on observed functional impairment rather than muscle strength grading alone. Although dorsiflexion strength measurements varied across assessments, both assessments identified neurological impairment affecting ankle control during gait. Functional assessment consistently demonstrated foot drop, reduced toe clearance, increased falls risk, and decreased walking efficiency. The proposed device directly addresses these impairments and supports achievement of the participant's mobility goals.

NDIS 심사관 관점

오히려 이렇게 작성하면 더 강해집니다.

왜냐하면:

"0/5인데 왜 움직이냐?"라는 의문 방지
"3/5면 AFO나 FES가 필요 없는 것 아닌가?"라는 의문 방지
"근력 수치가 아니라 기능적 보행장애(functional foot drop)가 핵심 문제" 라는 점을 강조할 수 있음

따라서 Assessment Findings, Trial Outcomes, Clinical Justification 3개 섹션만 수정하면 충분하고, 나머지 레포트는 그대로 유지해도 됩니다. 특히 최종 제출본에서는 0/5 또는 3/5 중 하나를 삭제하지 말고 두 결과를 모두 기재한 후 차이를 설명하는 방식이 가장 방어력이 높습니다.
