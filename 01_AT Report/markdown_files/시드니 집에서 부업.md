📄 NDIS Support Letter 템플릿 (Orthotic / Clinical)

[Provider Letterhead]

Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

Re: NDIS Support Request – [Participant Full Name]
NDIS Number: [XXXXXXXXX]
Date of Birth: [DD/MM/YYYY]

1. Introduction

I am writing in my capacity as a [Your Role – e.g., Orthotist] involved in the clinical management of [Participant Name]. This letter is to support the request for funding of [Requested Support – e.g., Bilateral Solid Ankle Foot Orthoses (SAFOs)] under the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS).

2. Background and Diagnosis

[Participant Name] has a diagnosis of [Diagnosis – e.g., Cerebral Palsy], which results in functional limitations including:

Reduced balance and postural stability
Altered gait pattern
Muscle weakness and/or spasticity
Increased risk of falls

These impairments significantly impact their ability to safely mobilise and participate in daily activities.

3. Current Functional Presentation

On recent clinical review dated [DD/MM/YYYY], the following was observed:

[e.g., Reduced ankle stability bilaterally]
[e.g., Excessive hip and knee flexion during swing phase for foot clearance]
[e.g., Left ankle plantarflexion affecting initial contact]
[e.g., Left calf tightness impacting ankle range of motion]

As a result, [Participant Name] demonstrates inefficient gait, increased energy expenditure, and reduced safety during ambulation.

4. Clinical Need and Justification

Due to the above findings, [Participant Name] requires [Requested Support – e.g., Bilateral SAFOs] to:

Improve ankle and foot alignment
Enhance stability during stance phase
Reduce compensatory gait patterns
Minimise risk of falls and injury
Support safe and efficient mobility

Additionally, it is recommended that the existing [e.g., Left AFO] be repaired and retained for occasional use where increased mobility or flexibility is required.

5. Expected Outcomes

Provision of the recommended orthoses is expected to:

Improve gait efficiency and safety
Increase independence in daily mobility
Reduce fatigue and risk of secondary complications
Support participation in home, school, and community environments
6. NDIS Criteria Alignment

The requested supports are considered:

Reasonable and necessary to achieve functional goals
Directly related to the participant’s disability
Evidence-based and clinically justified
Cost-effective, as they reduce the need for higher-level supports
7. Ongoing Needs and Review

Children and individuals with neurological conditions require periodic reassessment due to growth, changes in muscle tone, range of motion, and interventions such as Botox. These factors may affect orthotic fit and function over time.

8. Conclusion

Based on clinical assessment, it is my professional opinion that the provision of [Requested Support] is essential to support [Participant Name]’s safety, mobility, and functional independence.

Please do not hesitate to contact me should further information be required.

Kind regards,

[Your Name]
[Your Title – Orthotist / Clinician]
[Provider Name]
[Contact Details]

💡 바로 적용 팁

이 템플릿은 이렇게 쓰면 좋아요:

✔️ 상황에 맞게 커스터마이즈
