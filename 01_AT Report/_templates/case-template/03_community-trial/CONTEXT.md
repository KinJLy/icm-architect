# 03_community-trial — run and record the trial

One job: collect trial evidence (logbook + outcome measures) that will support the funding case.

## Inputs
- Working (this run): ../02_trial-request/output/support-letter.md (confirms what's being trialled and why)
- Reference (every run): ../../../references/community-trial-protocol.md (the 8-week phase structure + baseline checklist)
- Reference (every run): ../../../references/outcome-measures.md

## Process
1. Baseline (Week 0): complete every measure in the baseline checklist before the device is fitted.
2. Weeks 1–8: follow the four phases in `community-trial-protocol.md` (adaptation → functional expansion → validation → evidence collection). Keep the participant's daily-use diary — it's some of the strongest NDIS evidence available.
3. Re-run the outcome measures at the midpoint and end of trial.

## Outputs
- logbook.md → output/ (participant diary entries, kept close to verbatim)
- outcome-measures.md → output/ (baseline vs end-of-trial table)

## Human check
Treating clinician reviews outcome measures for plausibility and completeness before they're cited in the report.
